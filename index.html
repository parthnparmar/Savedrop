Daily Challenges

At Home

  • Fix leaky faucets - saves up to 3,000 gallons/year
  • Take shorter showers - save 5 gallons per minute
  • Turn off tap while brushing teeth
  • Use dishwasher only when full

Bathroom

  • Install low-flow showerheads
  • Use dual-flush toilets
  • Collect shower water while warming up
  • Take 5-minute showers

Kitchen

  • Wash vegetables in a bowl, not running water
  • Reuse pasta water for plants
  • Run full loads in dishwasher
  • Keep drinking water in fridge

Garden

  • Water plants early morning or evening
  • Use drip irrigation systems
  • Collect rainwater for gardening
  • Choose drought-resistant plants
